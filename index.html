Discover the Spirituality, Architectural Marvels, Wild Wonders

Spiritual Splendor

Explore the ancient temples of Puri, Bhubneswar, Jajpur and Sambalpur including the iconic Shri Jagannath Temple, Lingaraj Temple, Biraja Temple and Samaleswri Temple.

Wild Wonders

Immerse yourself in the lush greenery of Bhitarkanika National Park, a haven for wildlife enthusiasts. Discover the only place in the world that is the nesting ground for Olive Ridley Turtle and experience the Nesting Event.

Architechtural Marvels

Marvel at the breathtaking Sun Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and stroll along the tranquil Chandrabhaga Beach. Konark's architectural prowess and coastal beauty await your exploration.
